About Clubnow

Clubnow was founded on the principle that modern living demands both **excellence in performance** and **elegant design**. We are not just an appliance retailer; we are curators of future-proof homes. We rigorously select products that adhere to the highest standards of energy efficiency, sustainability, and technological innovation.

Our unique Club model provides exclusive pricing to registered members, ensuring you get premium technology without the premium markup. We partner directly with leading global manufacturers to streamline the supply chain and pass those savings directly to you. Our commitment is simple: **elevated living, accessible to everyone.**

We are a customer-centric team operating from the heart of the tech revolution, dedicated to supporting your journey to a smarter, more beautiful home.

Our Story: From Concept to Club

The journey of Clubnow began in 2021 when our founder, an appliance engineer, noticed a significant gap in the market: consumers had to choose between cutting-edge, expensive appliances and affordable, outdated ones. The idea of an "**Appliance Club**" was born—a model designed to democratize high-end home technology.

We spent a year establishing direct relationships with key manufacturers and building a lean, digital-first operation. By focusing on efficiency and membership, we successfully launched Clubnow, immediately disrupting the traditional retail model. Every appliance you see today has a story of innovation, quality, and rigorous testing behind it, reflecting our dedication to not just selling products, but enabling better lifestyles.
